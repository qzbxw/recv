Checkout: The Gold Standard of Crypto UX

The checkout page is the conversion layer between your cart and the customer's wallet. It explains the amount, network, QR, address and live payment state without sending the customer to pricing or plan details.

1

Customer opens a hosted checkout link from your store or bot.

2

recv locks the amount, network and recipient address.

3

Customer scans QR or copies the address into a wallet.

4

Watchers detect the transfer and move the order through the payment states.

5

Your system receives a settled result and can fulfill the order.

Payment states

Awaiting payment

Invoice is open and the QR/address are ready.

Detected

A matching transaction is seen before final confirmation.

Underpaid

Partial amount is received and the remaining balance is shown.

Paid

Required amount is confirmed and the invoice can be fulfilled.

Expired

The payment window has closed and the address should not be reused.

recv Checkout is a non-custodial payment gateway engineered for high-conversion cryptocurrency processing. Designed to eliminate friction in digital commerce, the UI supports direct-to-wallet transactions across TON, TRON (TRC-20), Base, and BSC-compatible networks. Core functionality includes dynamic exchange rate calculation, deep linking to major mobile wallets, and intelligent underpayment resolution to recover partial transactions. By bypassing intermediary custodians, merchants retain full control of their liquidity while providing customers with a seamless, multi-chain payment experience without turnover fees.
