Protocol

Direct-to-Wallet Architecture Explained

recv operates as a transparent middleware. Transactions flow directly from client to merchant, bypassing intermediary accounts.

Direct Settlement

recv does not hold merchant funds. Payments settle to your configured wallets while you remain responsible for customer, tax, and compliance requirements.

0% Turnover Commission

recv uses subscription pricing rather than charging a percentage of payment volume. Network fees still apply to blockchain transfers.

Telegram Alerts

Connect the Telegram bot to receive notifications when invoices and confirmed payments are recorded.

Smart Underpayment Handling

Our checkout interface automatically detects if a user sends less than the required amount, prompting them to send the remaining balance seamlessly.
